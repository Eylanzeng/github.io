Steps

  1. Apply butter on the wall of the cup in advance, and then pour an appropriate amount of sugar evenly so that the sugar evenly covers the inner wall and then pour it out. This is to allow the soufflé to expand smoothly during the baking process.
  2. Mix milk and butter and heat, bring to a slight boil, pour in sifted low-gluten flour, stir quickly and cook until 82°C, remove from heat, sift evenly.
  3. Lower the temperature, add the egg yolks and eggs into the egg liquid, and mix until the texture is fine and even.
  4. Add sugar to the egg whites in 3 batches to beat the meringue. After beating until wet peaks form, take part of the meringue and mix evenly with the batter, then pour it back into the remaining meringue and mix evenly.
  5. After injecting into the mold, smooth the surface and clean the inner wall with your fingers to make it easier for the soufflé to expand. Bake in an open oven at 175°C for about 20 minutes. After coming out of the oven, sift an appropriate amount of powdered sugar on the surface.
  6. Ready to eat
